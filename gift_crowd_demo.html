About Gift Crowd
Too many toys, not enough time, and a gift nobody was sure about. Sound familiar?
Gift Crowd makes it easy for parents to give together - simple, low-pressure, and done in minutes.
How it works
1
Create your party page. Add your child's name, the date, and their birthday wish.
2
Send the invite. A short WhatsApp message with a link. Guests RSVP on the page and see the gift option once they've confirmed.
3
Guests choose freely. They can participate in the group gift or simply come and celebrate. Both options are always equally visible.
4
Contributions are anonymous. Nobody knows who gave what.
5
Ask a friend to take the lead (optional). Pass the gift share message to someone you trust - they send it to the group, like a proper gift champion!
A group gift can also be...
🌱 A friendship garden
Each guest brings a small plant or seed packet. Together, everyone plants a friendship garden - a living reminder of the people who came to celebrate.
📚 A book swap
Guests bring a favourite book and take one home from the birthday child's collection. Stories shared, stories discovered.
🍎 Food for a food bank
Each guest brings a tin or packet of food. After the party, everyone delivers it to the local food bank together.
🔧 Toy repair workshop
Guests bring a broken toy. A repair expert helps children fix and upcycle them - creativity, sustainability, and a lot of fun.
FAQ
How do I set a contribution limit?
When setting up the gift, you choose a maximum amount per person. Guests can contribute anything up to that amount - or nothing at all.
Will guests know what others contributed?
No. Contributions are anonymised by default. Neither the host nor other guests can see individual amounts - only the total collected.
What if more money is raised than needed?
Any surplus can go to a charity of your child's choice.
What if a guest prefers to just come and celebrate?
Guests are free to join the group gift or simply show up and enjoy the party.
